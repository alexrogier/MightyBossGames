Level up how you play D&D

Our item cards are going to change how you manage your character’s equipment. Each card contains everything you need to know about the item in a clearly written and easily understandable format. Tracking your equipment with cards enables you to manage your character's inventory significantly easier. You will also be able to reference your equipment's rules in record time compared to sifting through a character sheet.

Unleash the dragon’s horde! Introducing new equipment into your games is exciting! Explore each of our items and surely you’ll find many that you will want to loot for yourself. We plan to produce cards in sets with each set tailored to specific themes of items. Rogues will rejoice with a Skullduggery set theme, Monks and Fighters let loose a warcry with a Martial set theme and there’s more in development.

Want a proof of concept on what we plan to produce? Checkout the images below and preview our item card design. If you’d like to provide any feedback regarding our design, send us an email at contact@mightybossgames.com or reach out to us on social media. We’ll be eager to hear what you think!

About Mighty Boss Games

We are currently a couple of guys from Indiana, USA with the aspiration of producing tabletop gaming products. Tabletop gaming is a big passion of ours which we indulge in almost every genre from board games, role playing games, collectible card games, wargaming and so on. Both of us have been playing tabletop games our entire lives. We’re also running a longstanding D&D campaign that’s now achieved its 6th year anniversary!

As Mighty Boss Games we want to make awesome products that will enhance someone’s experience when they play tabletop games. Since we're a startup, we thought the best way to get started was to produce item cards for D&D 5e. We hope to invest our success from this first project into many future projects including producing more supplemental content for role playing games and even publishing our own board games. Our imagination is the limit, but we want to focus on making our first project a success before bogging ourselves down with too many ambitions.

Kickstarter is our platform of choice because we believe in the success of startups funding their dreams through crowdfunding.
