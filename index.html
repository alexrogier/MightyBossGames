Details

All of our items are designed to balance around player levels 5-15. An exception to this is the Cataclysm item set. Those items are nearly gamebreaking and should be reserved for high level of play or if you want to destroy campaigns! Otherwise each of our items follow the design pattern of D&D 5e's rules and systems in order to seamlessly work with other officially published items. Part of our design process was to ensure that we create interesting items and not those that only provide stat buffs. Some items will include stat buffs when warranted, but most items were made with thematic coolness and encourages creative usage by the player. Also each item features a unique illustration! No illustration is the same and none of the illustrations depict a generic item.

Other perks of the item cards include...

  • Sloppy handwritten effect descriptions
  • Boring "+1 Strength" items
  • Easy and accessible tracking of equipment information
  • Convenient with other accessories like character-folios and tomes
  • Immersive artwork on the back of every card
  • Great for DM's and players alike
  • Effect descriptions in multiple rulebooks
  • Forgetting item functions from online generators
  • Remove tracking burden from DMs
  • A wealthy amount of new content for your campaign
  • Epic items cool enough to center your campaign around

About Mighty Boss Games

We are a couple of guys from Indiana, USA with the aspiration of producing tabletop gaming products. Tabletop gaming is a big passion of ours which we indulge in almost every genre from board games, role playing games, collectible card games, wargaming and so on. Both of us have been playing tabletop games our entire lives. We’re also running a longstanding D&D campaign that’s now achieved its 6th year anniversary!

As Mighty Boss Games we want to make awesome products that will enhance someone’s experience when they play tabletop games. Since we're a startup, we thought the best way to get started was to produce item cards for D&D 5e. We hope to invest our success from this first project into many future projects including producing more supplemental content for role playing games and even publishing our own board games. Our imagination is the limit, but we want to focus on making our first project a success before bogging ourselves down with too many ambitions.

Kickstarter is our platform of choice because we believe in the success of startups funding their dreams through crowdfunding.
